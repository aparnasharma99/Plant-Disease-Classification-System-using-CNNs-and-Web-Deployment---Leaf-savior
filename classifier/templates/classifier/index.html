Welcome to Leaf Savior - AI Powered Plant Health Monitoring



The Plant Disease Detection Web Application! This platform is designed to help farmers, gardeners, and agricultural professionals quickly and accurately identify plant diseases from images of leaves. Using state-of-the-art deep learning model - EfficientNetB3, the app analyzes leaf images to detect over 26 different diseases across 14 plant species with a high degree of accuracy.



Built on a robust backend powered by the Django framework, our application provides real-time predictions with confidence scores, making it a valuable tool for timely and effective crop management.


Simply upload a photo of a leaf, and let our advanced AI models do the rest!
